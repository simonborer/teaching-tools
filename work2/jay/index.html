Rejouvernate

Rejourvernate is a fete that happens right before Caribana and is typically celebrated by throwing coloured powders and paints on each other and this is traditionally called a Jourvert across the Caribbean. This video by Ari Howell is quick depiction of what a Jouvert is. Jouvert 2017 (paint party) - Trinidad and Tobago

Carnival Kindom

This fete typically the night of Caribana after the big parade. This event is known for the high profile soca artiste that take the stage. Carnival Kingdom

Return Fete

Return Fete takes place right after Trinidad Carnival. This fete happens to welcome back all those who went to trinidad. Also Return fete is known for it's star studded cast of preformers.

Soca or Die

Soca or Die is the most infamous soca fete in Toronto. Everyone and anyone who loves Soca and Caribbean culture has been to this event. This event has been attended by the likes of numerous NBA players like Lebron James and Rap star Cardi B
